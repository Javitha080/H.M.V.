About Our School

Nurturing minds, shaping futures

Homagama Maha Vidyalaya, located on Athurugiriya Road in Homagama, Colombo district, is a prominent educational institution under the Western Province of Sri Lanka. With its long-standing reputation for academic excellence and holistic development, the school serves as a cornerstone for the community.

Established decades ago, Homagama Maha Vidyalaya has grown from humble beginnings into a thriving educational hub. The school offers classes from Grades 1 to 11, catering to students across various age groups with a student population of 2,376—comprising 1,221 male and 1,155 female students.

Our curriculum is designed to balance academic rigor with extracurricular engagement, ensuring students receive a well-rounded education. We focus on developing not just knowledge, but also character, leadership, and social responsibility.
